Kado

Kado is a website that allows you to compile a wishlist with items from all over the web. Kado was created using Django, Javascript, Beautiful Soup, AWS, some Twitter Bootstrap, and hosted on Heroku. When you add an entry to your list, Kado scrapes the item's page and grabs pictures for your choosing. Below are a couple of screenshots that take you throught the flow of adding an item to your list. This project has a lot of little bugs that I might get around to fixing in the future but for now, I'm more interested in other projects.
